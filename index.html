Urban Flood Management with Real-Time Monitoring

AI & IoT Based Smart Drainage Monitoring System

Problem Statement

Urban flooding is increasing due to rapid urbanization and inefficient drainage systems. Traditional manual inspections are slow, risky, and reactive. The absence of predictive monitoring and early warning systems leads to delayed responses and increased hazards.

Proposed Solution

Our system integrates IoT sensors and AI to continuously monitor water levels, toxic gases, and blockages in drainage systems. Real-time data is visualized on a dashboard, analyzed using machine learning, and automated email alerts are sent to officials when danger thresholds are crossed.
